My Journey

Helping Clients Achieve Optimal Health Through Nutrition

As a registered dietitian with over 5 years of experience, I'm passionate about translating nutritional science into practical, sustainable eating strategies. My approach goes beyond temporary diets to create lasting lifestyle changes that support your unique health goals and preferences.

I hold a Master's degree in Nutritional Sciences and am board certified in several specialty areas. My practice combines evidence-based recommendations with compassionate, individualized care to help clients navigate the often confusing world of nutrition information.

View My Services

Mission

To empower individuals with science-based nutrition knowledge and practical tools that enable them to make informed food choices supporting lifelong health and wellbeing.

Vision

A world where nutrition education is accessible to all, and where food is recognized as both preventive medicine and a source of joy and connection.

Philosophy

Nutrition should be personalized, not prescriptive. I believe in a non-diet approach that honors both physical health and mental wellbeing through balanced, flexible eating patterns.
